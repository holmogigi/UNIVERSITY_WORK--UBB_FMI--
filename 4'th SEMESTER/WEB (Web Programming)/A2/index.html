A little about himself

Christopher Adam Bumstead (born 2 February 1995) is a Canadian IFBB professional bodybuilder. Bumstead is the reigning Mr. Olympia Classic Physique winner, having won the competition in 2019, 2020, 2021 and 2022. He was also the runner-up in 2017 and 2018. Bumstead maintains a large online presence with content focusing on his lifestyle and bodybuilding.
He is often referred to by his nickname Cbum.
